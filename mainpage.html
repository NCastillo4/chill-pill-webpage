Chill Pill helps you schedule reminders and maintain your daily vitamin and medicince intake. One stop shop for individuals or your family

How it works

Sign Up

Sign up via email or phone number to get started

Add Items

Start adding items in the app by photographing or uploading the item label using your cell phone. The photograph will pre-fill item details to save you time!

Edit & Set Reminders

You’re able to edit any details, alert time(s) and sharing options at this stage or later on. Whatever works best for you!

All Set!

After you’ve entered your first item, you can keep add items as you need. You can also add people to your inner circle that you may want to either be informed of your medication or theirs.

Connect

Chill Pill aims to grow with our users and their needs. As life’s circumstances change or your family circle grows we’d like to help our users stay connected by being able to add, send or receive alerts to and from members of their inner circle.
